About us

Sypher Labs Pte Ltd was incorporated on 21 August 2012 by CEO & founder Shamir Rahim. Sypher Labs is currently incubated by NUS Enterprise and is supported by SPRING Singapore via the ACE Startups Grant. The company is also a consortium member of the IDA Geospatial call-for-proposal.

The Sypher Labs team has over 10 years’ experience in setting up and maintaining office information systems for logistics SMEs, and are thus acutely familiar with the strategic challenges and day-to-day operational issues they face.

Sypher Labs’ network of logistics SMEs, including its current pipeline customers, extend to Johor Bahru, Kuala Lumpur, Ipoh and Penang. It is looking to expand to Thailand and Indonesia as well in the near future.
